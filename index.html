How to Play

Use WASD to move

Press space to use selected tool

Click tools to select them

Sustainability Tips

• Beans improve soil quality

• Corn reduces carbon but depletes soil

• Wheat has balanced impact

Crop Rotation

• Plant corn after beans (+50% value)

• Plant wheat after corn (+30% value)

• Plant beans after wheat (+20% value)

• Avoid planting same crop twice (-30% to -10% value)
